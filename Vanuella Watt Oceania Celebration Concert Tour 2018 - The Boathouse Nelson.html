Vanuella Watt Oceania Celebration Concert Tour 2018 🗓

Thursday 22nd November 2018, 8pm (doors open from 7.30pm)

The Alliances Françaises of Nelson-Tasman, Auckland, Palmerston North and Christchurch, together with the Government of New Caledonia, the Delegation of New Caledonia to New Zealand, the Pacific community, Aircalin, New Caledonia Tourism and Maison Vauron Wine Merchants are proud to present New Caledonian singer Vanuella Watt, the ‘Pacific Diva’ who will be travelling to New Zealand from 14th to 24th November.

Vanuella will be in Nelson at the newly restored Boathouse for a very special concert, introducing her famous album “Oceania: a Hymn for the Pacific” for the first time in New Zealand! The New Caledonian diva will also entertain us with a beautiful repertoire of her own original compositions, classic French chansons, Pacific songs and world-famous songs in several languages, encompassing many styles and genres.

Tickets through EventBrite ,Beggs in Hardy Street or door sales on the night from The Boathouse.
$15 tickets for AF member you can buy your ticket for $15 from Beggs by showing your ID upon purchase or email afntsecretary@gmail.com after you have deposited $15 into our bank account.

November 22, 2018 from 8:00 pm to 10:30 pm
